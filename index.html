Life and Consumption Experience Global Instant Sharing Platform

OUR PRODUCT

Globalized social functioning design

As the first globalized instant sharing tool with accurate classification of social information, UFree aims to gather users worldwide by satisfying their desires of sharing different life and consumption experience.

Double social links

Acquaintances social patterns will be combined with expanding social patterns under the uniform system. It contains IM in friends circle and infinite resource searching in communities. Users can enjoy the customization of social space.

Category search based on clusters information

All posts can be labelled as 11 fields of consumption experience of people. We provide efficient category searching strategies for users to practice interest matching.

Filtering mechanism and information flow customization

Users have self-determined options to information flow recommendation. They decide their social domain, ways and depth of social behaviors with free will. Junk information could be intercepted by filtering mechanism of platform.

MAGIC FETURES

Decentralized algorithm of tracking and recommending Information flows

The value and degree of sharing of a single message will be the core indicators of information flows recommendation. Each real and valuable message from users has comparatively equal opportunity to be gripped and recommended. Real words of users instead of internet authorities supported by commercial capitals impact on users’ behaviors.
