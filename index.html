OUR PRODUCT

Globalized function design based on generality of mankind

As the first globalized instant sharing tool with accurate classification of social information, UFREE aims to attract users worldwide by satisfying their common desires of sharing different life and consumption experience.

Double social links

The acquaintances social links will be combined with the expanding stranger social links to be the uniform system. It contains Instant Messaging in friends circle and infinite information resources searching in classified communities. All users can enjoy the customization of social information space.

Category search based on clusters information

All posts can be labelled as 10 fields of life and consumption experience of people. We provide efficient category searching strategies for users to realize interests matching, information integration and intelligent cooperation.

Filtering mechanism and information flow customization

Users have self-determined options to information flow recommendation. They decide their social domain, ways and depth of social behaviors with free will. Junk information could be intercepted by filtering mechanism of platform.

MAGIC FEATURES

Decentralized algorithm of tracking and recommending Information flows

The value and degree of sharing of a single message will be the core indicators of information flows recommendation. Real words of users instead of advertising impact on users' decisions. The consensus social credit system is designed as the foundation for publicly verifying the authenticity of information and resources. The information dissemination and integration paths are traceable and open to be reviewed.
